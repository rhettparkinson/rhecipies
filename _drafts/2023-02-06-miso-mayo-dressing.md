{% ingredient %}
- ¼ tsp ginger, grated
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup white miso paste
- ¼ tsp soy sauce
- ½ lemon, juiced
{% endingredient %}

{% method %}
- Peel and grate the ginger to yield ¼ tsp.
- In a large mixing bowl, add mayonnaise, white miso paste, ginger, soy sauce and juice of ½ lemon.
- Mix until it is well combined.
{% endmethod %}
